Lattice — Privacy Policy

Effective June 10, 2026 · Drylabs GmbH

The short version

Lattice works offline and needs no account. We run no ads, no analytics SDKs, and no trackers. Your puzzle progress stays on your device.

What leaves your device

Opt-in leaderboard: if you choose "Post to today's board" in Endless, we send your chosen display name, the day's seed, and your move list to our server, which recomputes your score and stores name + score for up to 14 days. This is not linked to your identity — we hold no accounts, no emails, no device identifiers.

Daily verification: solved daily boards may be verified against our server; the request contains only the puzzle and your placements — no identifiers.

What we never collect

Name, email, location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, usage analytics. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple; we never see your payment details.

Children

Lattice is rated 4+ and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.
